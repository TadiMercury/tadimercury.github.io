About Us

About BCA

The word Batanai means “togetherness” and implies that together our clients and ourselves we are partners in the corporate world of business.

We will become a formidable factor when we jointly work together for your objectives.

Who are we?

We are a team of highly qualified and highly skilled individuals who are motivated to deliver client services and satisfy your own, and your business needs.

Batanai Corporate Advisory (BCA) has been established as an emerging black-owned Accounting and Consultancy firm. We specialise in bringing integrity to the accounting and auditing profession, by delivering integrated information which clients can use to run their business with sustainable reporting. As much as we are confident of our technical and professional expertise in bringing solutions to our clients, we also partner with other firms for capacity building and skills transfer in complex and large assignments. Our partners are carefully chosen to maintain our core values and sustainable service in business.

Given this background, we are confident that we not only have the required expertise in providing professional and value adding accounting and audit services to our clients, but will also stay focused on our commitment in maintaining our broad objectives.

Why BCA?

We’re here to listen and to understand and share your passion for your business and to use our expertise and business acumen to help your organisation achieve its goals.

BCA understands that both public and private sector entities need guidance to help manage their organisations, establish robust internal controls, use information technology effectively and improve performance.

The team at BCA can provide you with an objective, unbiased and comprehensive view of your situation and deliver value-added solutions that will enable you to create sound strategies, increase technological capabilities and improve your operational efficiency. We work with you to understand your issues and your interests. Our systematic approach, customised to meet your unique needs, focuses on improving your business performance.

At Batanai Corporate Advisory, our approach reflects our experience and demonstrates an understanding of what our clients need.

Our range of audit and accountancy services offers clients reassurance from a technical perspective, enthusiasm and responsiveness. Each audit is headed up by a dedicated partner and manager team whose job it is to ensure we deliver what we promise.

Your audit partner will be expertly placed to put you in touch with the many specialist services you may need as your business grows.
